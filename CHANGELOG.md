# Changelog

## 1.0.0 (2026-06-16)

### Features
- 💱 **Currency Formatting**: Format PHP amounts with Intl.NumberFormat, parse formatted strings, format in words
- 🔄 **Multi-Currency Conversion**: 30+ world currencies with exchange rate management
- 📊 **VAT Calculations**: Standard (12%), reduced, zero-rated, exempt VAT with add/remove/breakdown utilities
- 📊 **Withholding Tax**: Regular, final, and expanded withholding tax calculations per BIR rates
- 🏛️ **Income Tax**: TRAIN Law / CREATE Act brackets, monthly withholding, annualization, BIR Form 2316
- 🏦 **Government Contributions**: SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG (HDMF) calculations
- 💰 **Denomination Breakdown**: Split amounts into Philippine bills and coins
- ✅ **Validation**: Amount, currency code, exchange rate, tax rate validation
- 📋 **Constants**: All tax rates, exchange rates, denominations, and contribution rates
- 🎯 **TypeScript**: Full type definitions, strict mode, declaration files
- ✅ **Tests**: 161 comprehensive tests with coverage

### Tax Rates
- VAT: 12% standard, 5% reduced
- Income tax: 6 brackets (TRAIN Law / CREATE Act)
- Withholding tax: 11 types
- Final withholding tax: 11 types
- Expanded withholding tax: 9 types
- SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG contribution rates
